**Riddle Help**
<<>><<>><<>><<>><<>><<>><<>><<>><<>>
`!riddle get` get a riddle.
`!riddle get current` redisplay last riddle you got.
`!riddle solve RIDDLE.ID ||SOLUTION||` solve a riddle.
`!riddle score` get your score.
`!riddle leaderboard NUMBER` get the top X users, or leave blank for top 10.
`!riddle add TITLE: TEXT ||SOLUTION||` add a new riddle.
