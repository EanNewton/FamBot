Please be patient when using these commands, they may take a moment.
`!stats` Display a word cloud for the server.
`!stats user USERNAME` Display a word cloud for the specified user.
`!stats channel CHANNEL` Display a word cloud for the specified channel.
`!stats count LOW HIGH` Display count of messages of word specified number of words, LOW and HIGH are optional.
`!stats phrases LOW HIGH LIMIT` Display the most common phrases of specified length, LOW HIGH and LIMIT are optional.
`!stats common LIMIT` Display a text list of the most common single words and their counts.
