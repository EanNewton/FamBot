**Stats Help**
<<>><<>><<>><<>><<>><<>><<>><<>><<>>
Please be patient when using these commands. 
They require a considerable amount of processing and may take a moment.
`!stats` get a word cloud for the server.
`!stats count LOW HIGH` get count of messages of word specified number of words, LOW and HIGH are optional.
`!stats phrases LOW HIGH LIMIT` get the most common phrases of specified length, LOW HIGH and LIMIT are optional.
`!stats user USERNAME` get a word cloud for the specified user.
`!stats channel CHANNEL` get a word cloud for the specified channel.
`!stats common LIMIT` get a text list of the most common single words and their counts.
